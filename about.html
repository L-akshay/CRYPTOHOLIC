About Cryptoholic

Our Story

Cryptoholic emerged as India's pioneering decentralized Web 3.0 society, with a vision to bridge the gap between traditional technology education and the evolving landscape of blockchain, AI, and metaverse technologies.

Founded by passionate tech enthusiasts, we've created a vibrant community where innovation meets education, enabling the next generation of tech leaders to thrive in the decentralized future.

Campus Expansion & Impact

Expanding Our Reach

Cryptoholic is expanding across prestigious institutions such as Delhi University, Uttarakhand Technical University, Daulat Ram College, Shyam Lal College, and Tulas Institute of Dehradun, fostering collaboration and hands-on learning in Web3 and blockchain.

We bridge student communities with the global Web 3.0 ecosystem, offering opportunities for networking and real-world project experience.

Financial & Educational Impact

Our members have collectively earned over ₹2 Lakh through airdrops, and many students have participated in paid playtests, turning their passion for technology into financial gains.

Expert sessions with industry leaders like KGen, Game7, and Lysto provide insights into Web3 gaming and the transition from Web2 to Web3.
