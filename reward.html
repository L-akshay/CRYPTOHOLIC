Cryptoholic Society Rewards

Introduction

Cryptoholic Society is India's first decentralized Web 3.0 society, offering education on blockchain, AI, digital currencies, NFTs, and the Metaverse. Our aim is to equip members with knowledge of these innovative technologies while creating networking opportunities with global Web 3.0 companies.

This page outlines our Participation Points System, Rewards, and Removal Policy. Let's foster healthy competition, accountability, and active involvement within our thriving Web 3.0 community.

Objectives

  • Promote Active Participation
  • Reward Positive Contributions
  • Maintain Accountability
  • Create Opportunities for Recognition
  • Foster Healthy Competition

Participation Points System

Our token reward system motivates and rewards active involvement and teamwork within the community. Here's how you can earn points:

  • Online Meetings
    • Attend Online Meet: +1 point
    • Random Snapshot of Meet: +1 point
    • Q&A Session (Positive Response): +2 points
  • Offline Meetings
    • Attend Offline Meet: +1 point
    • Work Performance (Positive Response): +10 or +5 points
  • Work Performance Bonuses
    • Nice Work (General): +3 points
    • Nice Work (Offline): +5 points
    • Sponsored Collaboration (Company/Project): +30 points
